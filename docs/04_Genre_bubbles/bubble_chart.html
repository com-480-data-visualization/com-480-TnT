About

This visualization displays the most popular genres of music in the world, based on Spotify data. The size of each bubble represents the number of albums in that genre.

Click on a bubble to see the sub genres of that genre. Clicking again will show common albums in that sub genre.

Click on an album to see more details, including a Spotify embed to listen to a quick preview of the album!

Browse through the genres and discover the diversity of music. And if you feel like it, you can take a quick listen, may be you will find your next favorite album!
