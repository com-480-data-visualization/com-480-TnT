Genre and subgenre exploration

About

This visualization invites you to explore the intricate relationships between music genres and their subgenres. The Sankey diagram visually maps out how major genres branch into their respective subgenres, offering insights into the diversity and evolution of musical styles, as well as the cross-influences between different genres. It serves as a dynamic overview of how music has grown, blended, and diversified over time.

Hover over any node to highlight its connections and reveal the directional flow between genres and subgenres. This interaction helps you trace how certain styles have influenced others and how interconnected the musical landscape is.
